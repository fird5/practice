2. Massive Partnerships

Goal: Build and own the conversation in our key ecosystems.

3. Online Games (Twice a Month)

Goal: Be a constant, active presence, not a "one-off" event.

  • More university outreach

4. Always Inch Closer to Education

Goal: Be the most valuable educational resource in our niche.
