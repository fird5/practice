AI Esports Malaysia Varsity Challenge 2025

— Absurd Ingenuity Showcase —

The Game Structure: Absurdity Meets AI Execution

These challenges are designed to test the limits of **human creativity, multimodal integration, and constraint-driven ingenuity** in a rapid sprint. Success depends on how well you combine abstract concepts (like Nasi Lemak and orbital mechanics) into a cohesive, convincing visual product.

1. Hyper-Local Data Synthesis: The "Teh Tarik Economy" Index

Focus: Visualize national economic stability using only two metrics: the price fluctuation of tea leaves and the average "pull distance" (tarik height) of Teh Tarik vendors.

Lateral Input: **Design Rule: The 'Obscure Correlation' Rule.** Your solution must create a statistical index where abstract, non-financial data proves a complex economic truth.

Output Focus: A single, high-impact visual that maps the **Obscure Correlation** (e.g., a volatility graph made of stretching tea).

2. Ethical & Philosophical Prototyping: The "Perfect Memory Curator" Device

Focus: Design a consumer tool that allows users to permanently delete bad memories, but the tool is powered only by consuming memories of *others*. How do you market it ethically?

Lateral Input: **Design Rule: The 'Zero-Sum Paradox' Rule.** The solution must justify an ethical system where the user's gain is directly predicated on a visible, quantifiable loss by the collective.

Output Focus: A visually compelling **"Ethical Flow Diagram"** (Blueprint) showing the transfer of memories and a **Tagline** that resolves the moral paradox (e.g., "Forget with Conscience").

3. Constraint-Driven Architecture: The "Kuala Lumpur Air Traffic Snail"

Focus: Design a new, safer air traffic control system for KLIA that is physically embodied by a giant, slow, mechanical snail moving across the city skyline. It must function perfectly.

Lateral Input: **Design Rule: The 'Deliberate Slowness' Rule.** The main component of the solution must be deliberately slow, forcing the rest of the system to become hyper-efficient in response.

Output Focus: A **3D model/Live-Generated Visual** of the functional snail structure controlling traffic, and an **Animated Loop** showing the *hyper-efficient flow* the slowness creates.

4. Absurd Educational Science: The "Quantum Entanglement Rendang"

Focus: Explain the concept of quantum entanglement (particles linked regardless of distance) to a primary school student using only the ingredients and flavor profiles of beef Rendang.

Lateral Input: **Design Rule: The 'Flavor Link' Rule.** The scientific concept must be visualized purely through the sensory connection of a complex, shared meal.

Output Focus: An **Animated Loop** showing two pieces of Rendang instantly adopting the same "flavor state" when separated by distance, and a **Vibe-Coded Presentation Deck** explaining the flavor analogy.

5. Emotional Urban Planning: The "Certified City of Introverts" Zoning Plan

Focus: Design the urban zoning plan for a city designed *exclusively* for introverts, where zoning laws are based on noise sensitivity and required personal space (not commerce or residence).

Lateral Input: **Design Rule: The 'Psychological Buffer' Rule.** The entire architecture must be structured around maximizing personal emotional space and minimizing required social exposure.

Output Focus: A **Live-Generated Visual** of the Vibe-Coded City Zoning Map (e.g., "The Quiet Zone," "The Scheduled Interaction Plaza"), and a **User Journey Narrative** showing an introvert successfully navigating the city with zero anxiety.
