Building AI Architects Across Southeast Asia

LAPA is a structured, gamified AI literacy programme for university students — combining weekly learning tracks, industrial sprints, and live esports-style tournaments into a single, repeatable academic experience.

Week 4 Tournament

The AI Esports Arena

A live-casted, publicly streamed 2-hour AI build tournament held online every month. All 10 qualifiers compete simultaneously — building in real time while audiences watch the action unfold.

The Format
2 hrs+
All 10 Compete Simultaneously
All ten monthly qualifiers receive the same Mystery Brief at the same moment and begin building in real time. No elimination rounds — everyone competes for the full duration, start to finish.
The Broadcast
Live
Casted & Streamed Publicly
The entire tournament is live-casted with commentary via LAPA's esports streaming platform — free to view for everyone. Audiences watch competitors ideate, prompt, and build in real time.
The Outcome
Live
Winners Announced On-Stream
Submissions are judged at the close of the session and results are announced live on stream. Monthly champions and top performers are recognised in front of the full public audience.

The Central Platform

Everything Runs
Through One App.

Every lesson, every sprint, every leaderboard point, every tournament qualifier — the entire LAPA ecosystem is orchestrated through a single platform. One login. Six months. No friction.

Learn
Snackable Micro-Lessons

Three 15-minute lessons per week — Vision, Tooling, Execution — delivered in-app on any device. Students learn at their own pace, locked into a structured weekly rhythm that builds competency module by module.

Compete
Live Leaderboard & Sprint Submissions

Every Industrial Sprint is submitted, judged, and scored directly through the app. Points accumulate on a live leaderboard visible to all participants — turning learning into a competitive, high-stakes performance sport.

Qualify
Tournament Access & Portfolio Gallery

The app closes the monthly leaderboard, notifies the Top 10 qualifiers, and hosts the verified In-App Gallery where every Sprint output is stored as a permanent, shareable portfolio entry.
