Milestones Tracker: How We Measure Success

These are the key indicators that tell us the flywheel is gaining momentum.

1. Super Visibility

  • 500 followers/subscribers on a new platform.
  • 1,000 organic views on a short-form video.
  • 1 traditional media publication.
  • 1 inbound lead from content ("I saw your...").

2. Massive Partnerships

  • 1 formal partner from each ecosystem (Esports, AI, Edu).
  • 1 co-branded event hosted with a partner.
  • 1 partner provides an expert for a webinar.
  • 1 partner renews their partnership.

3. Online Games

  • 1 inbound sponsorship request for a game.

4. Education

  • Host first webinar with 50+ live attendees.
  • Build a list of 10+ agreed-upon experts.
  • 1 educational institution shares our content.
  • Acquire a D_D "top-tier" expert.
