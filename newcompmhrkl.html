2. Create Quarterly Performance Bonus (QPB)

Instead of a standard annual increment that adds to Fixed Liabilities, MHRKL will shift toward a Quarterly Performance Bonus (QPB). This move ensures that personnel expenditure is only triggered by organizational success.

This encourages congruence of performance; staff understand that institutional health is a variable they directly influence through active fundraising stewardship and operational containment.

3. QPB decided by performance thresholds defined by surplus

Defining "Surplus"

Surplus is the realized cash balance remaining after all Fixed Operating Expenses are subtracted from Unrestricted Revenue (Donations, Grants, Indirect Cost Recoveries). Unlike a budget, surplus measures actual liquidity available for reserve replenishment.

Management Lever: Thresholds and Pool % Can Be Adjusted
Performance Tier Surplus Target Bonus Pool %
Survival Below $15,000 0% (Reserve Priority)
Base Performance $15,000 - $59,999 15.0% of Surplus
Superior Impact $60,000+ 20.0% of Surplus
