Key Terms
Clear terms.
No surprises.

A summary of the key conditions governing the LAPA University Partnership Agreement.

Subscription Fee
USD $0.99 / active user / month
Billed monthly in arrears based on verified active user count for that calendar month.
Active User Definition
Minimum 1 login per month
Any registered student who logs in at least once in the billing month. Inactive accounts are not billed.
Minimum Enrolment
500 students on-ramp
500-student on-ramp for the first 60 days. Minimum committed enrolment of 1,000 students from Month 3 onwards.
Agreement Duration
12 months
From date of first student activation. All pricing and revenue share provisions apply throughout the Initial Term.
Revenue Share
20% at 2,000+ active students
Remitted within 30 days of month-end. Designated exclusively for student scholarship purposes as determined by the Institution.
Pricing Protection
Rate locked for 12 months
The $0.99 rate is guaranteed for the full Initial Term. Renewal pricing communicated with 60 days' notice.
Revenue Share Eligibility
Monthly basis
Revenue share applies only to months where active users meet or exceed 2,000. Prior months do not carry forward.
Governing Entity
MICE Neurol Pte Ltd
Registered in Singapore. All commercial agreements governed under Singapore law unless otherwise agreed.
