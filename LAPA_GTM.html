Macro risk register
Critical
AI Literacy Commoditisation
Google, Microsoft, LinkedIn all pushing free AI content. Mitigation: LAPA's moat is gamification + certification layer, not content. Differentiate relentlessly.
Critical
University Activation Gap
MOUs ≠ revenue. SEA university bureaucracy stalls activation 6–12 months. Mitigation: Campus Reps + Student Union partnerships to drive bottom-up demand.
Critical
Curriculum Obsolescence
AI moves faster than any curriculum. Mitigation: Rapid refresh cycle baked into operations from day one. Modular content architecture.
High
Govt Endorsement Delays
Flywheel depends on MDEC/CHED. If delayed 18 months, mid-year acceleration fails. Mitigation: Flywheel must work without endorsement as base case.
High
Copycat Risk
Local edtech with govt connections could clone model. Mitigation: Speed of MOU signing is primary moat right now. Move faster than anyone can copy.
High
Founder Bandwidth
LAPA competing with beingremarkable.co, GeBiz, SkillsFuture advisory. Mitigation: Hard resource allocation decision needed. LAPA needs focused execution.
Medium
Pricing Mismatch
$12–15/month is meaningful for MY/PH students. Mitigation: HRD Corp in MY removes cost. Institutional pricing model separates student from university payment.
Medium
Student Drop-off
Gamification drives sign-ups but retention post-novelty is hard. Mitigation: Competition circuit + employer badge recognition create ongoing return incentives.
Medium
Credential Inflation
If badges are too easy, employers stop valuing them. Mitigation: Rigorous assessment layer required. Maintain difficulty bar even when it hurts enrolment numbers.
